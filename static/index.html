Quỹ ăn uống:

  1. Cat toc, cai nay tu giac nha. Sau 2 ngay ma khong tu giac, co nguoi phat giac la len bang 2 dong
  2. Noi bay, noi ko hop ly (tinh theo so dong dong tinh)
  3. Pass probation
  4. Sinh nhat
  5. Noi sai ten dong nghiep
  6. Dang order ma chat chen vao hoac co nhung cau hoi khong hop ly trong luc order
  7. An lon phan an cua nguoi khac
  8. Order ma quen an phan an cua minh
  9. Nhac nho nguoi khac lam soxes time lam that thu cua heo dat
  10. Xuc pham dong nghiep tren 2 nguoi nghe duoc
  11. De may tinh qua dem ma khong inform voi anh bo phan IT
  12. “Dính” phai nhung loi ma >= 1/2 so luong nguoi trong office dong y len bang
  13. Promotion
  14. Truoc khi ra ve phai dem ly nuoc lai nha bep. (chi tinh ly mau trang)

Ngoai sinh nhat, probation va cat toc thi moi dong tren bang tuong ung 200k

  1. Cat toc: 100k
  2. Promotion: Min 200k va Max Open
  3. Sinh nhat: Min 500K va Max Open... (Neu cty cho tien banh kem thi tien nay cung duoc sung vao cong qui, tom lai la den sinh nhat, nguoi do se phai ton it nhat 500k, 1 nguoi khoc de cho nhieu nguoi cuoi)
  4. Pass probation 500k doi voi junior, 1000k doi voi senior
  5. Thue thu nhap phat sinh khi nhan dc qua hoac bonus dac biet tu cty min 5%. (vi du nhu boc tham trung thuong, nhan vien cua nam….)
  6. Neu mua dien thoai, tablet, smart watch hoac xe moi se phai dong thue, Cong thuc tinh thue duoc cung cap boi Truc.
  7. Truoc khi ra ve phai dem ly nuoc lai nha bep: 10k/ly/ngay (vi du thu 6 ma de quen thi thu 2 len se co nguoi doi 30k tien Ly - CHI AP DUNG DOI VOI LY TRANG CUA CTY)

soXes time (cho “heo an”):

  1. Làm soXes time trễ hoặc sai => 10k/gio. Thoi gian duoc tinh tu luc 11h. (Vi du: Chi Ngoc bat soXes Time luc 2:30 => So tien phai “nop” heo la: 40k)
  2. Ko lam soXes time cua nhung ngay truoc do (cai nay ko lam nhung chi Ngoc ko co kiem tra hoac kiem tra nhung van ngoan co khong lam) => 10k/ngay.
